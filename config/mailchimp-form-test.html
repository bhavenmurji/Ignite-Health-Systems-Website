Quick Mailchimp Integration Test

To get your Mailchimp form action URL:

  1. Go to your Mailchimp dashboard
  2. Audience → Signup forms → Embedded forms
  3. Look for this line in the generated code:
    4. <form action="https://ignitehealthsystems.us[X].list-manage.com/subscribe/post?u=[USER_ID]&id=[LIST_ID]" method="post">
  4. Copy that entire URL and replace the action below
